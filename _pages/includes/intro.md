I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://en.sjtu.edu.cn/), under the supervision of Prof. [Cewu Lu](https://www.mvig.org/) and Prof. [Yong-Lu Li](https://dirtyharrylyl.github.io/) in the [RHOS](https://mvig-rhos.com/) group at [MVIG](https://www.mvig.org/).
Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree from [SJTU](https://en.sjtu.edu.cn/) in 2025.

My research interests lie in understanding and modeling the dynamic 2D and 3D physical world. I am especially interested in robotic scenarios, where perception, interaction, and physical reasoning are tightly coupled.
